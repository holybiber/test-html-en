Four Kinds of Disciples

Trainers

Share the gospel, start new groups and train them to repeat the process

Kingdom growth by multiplication

The VISION Barrier
inward focus – lack of generational foresight – too much control
Starters

Share the gospel, start new groups

Kingdom growth stops at 2nd generation

The PERMISSION Barrier
no permission to start new groups – pass disciple-making off to “specialists”
Witnesses

Share the gospel, take new people to existing groups

Kingdom growth by addition only

The CONFIDENCE Barrier
afraid to witness – make excuses – pass evangelism off to “gifted people”
Attenders

Don’t share, don’t start groups, don’t train others

No kingdom growth
