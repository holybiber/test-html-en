A Daily Prayer

Holy Spirit, fill me!

Pray for the Holy Spirit to work in you and through you.

Jesus, protect me!

Put yourself under the protection of Jesus' blood and sacrifice.

Heavenly Father, give me whatever gift I need today.

God can give you everything you'll need this day.

Thank you, God, for my life and everything you're giving me.

Say thanks in and for everything.


Put on the whole armor of God

  • Helmet of Salvation: God, protect and change my thoughts.
  • Breastplate of Righteousness: Thank you, God, for your grace by which I'm saved.
  • Belt of Truth: God, help me to speak truth and to live according to your truth.
  • Shoes of Readiness: God, help me to see situations where I can share about you and help me to do it.
  • Shield of Faith: God I trust you. Let my faith in you grow.
  • Sword / God's Word: God, help me to understand your word better and to use it.

(Ephesians 6:10-17)
